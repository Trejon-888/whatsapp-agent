## FAQ

**Q: Are there dairy-free options?**
A: Yes! We always have at least 2 sorbets (dairy-free) available — mango and a rotating flavor.

**Q: Do you do custom cakes?**
A: We do ice cream cakes! Starting at $35. Need at least 3 days notice. Message us to order.

**Q: Can I order for pickup?**
A: Absolutely. Send us your order here on WhatsApp and we'll have it ready when you arrive.

**Q: Do you deliver?**
A: We deliver within 5 miles of the shop for a $5 delivery fee. Minimum order $20.

**Q: Are you nut-free?**
A: Our shop handles nuts, so we can't guarantee a nut-free environment. We label all flavors with allergens though.
